Connect Your Telegram Channel or Group
Follow these steps to link your channel or group to ContentStudio. Once connected, you can schedule posts to it just like any other account.
✕
1
Open the ContentStudio bot on Telegram
Search for @contentstudio_bot on Telegram, or tap the button below to open it directly.
2
Add the bot as an administrator
Go to your channel or group Settings → Administrators → Add Administrator. Search for @contentstudio_bot and enable Post Messages permission.
3
Send the connection command
Copy the command below and send it inside your channel or group chat.
/connect CS-A4F2X9
