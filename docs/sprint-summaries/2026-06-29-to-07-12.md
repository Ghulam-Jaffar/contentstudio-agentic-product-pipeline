### ⚠️ Sentry noise note (CONT-2713 — Go analytics service)
Per PO, these are **expected conditions, not actionable errors — they must NOT be pushed to Sentry:**
- **Invalid / expired token** errors.
- **API-not-allowed / unavailable** failures — when the platform API simply isn't available for that account (commonly **low-audience** accounts where the metrics endpoint isn't permitted).

Recommend adding these two suppression rules as explicit acceptance criteria on CONT-2713 (handle/classify as warnings or drop, don't report to Sentry).
